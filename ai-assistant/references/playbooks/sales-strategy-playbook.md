# Sales Strategy Playbook

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

Use this when the task is about selling the product, improving pipeline quality, shaping the sales motion, or helping the team close revenue.

## Workflow

1. Identify the buyer, champion, user, and purchase trigger.
2. Clarify the value proposition in business terms, not just product features.
3. Map the likely sales motion for the current stage and ACV.
4. Identify proof points, objections, and friction in the buying journey.
5. Recommend messaging, assets, and experiments that shorten time-to-trust and time-to-close.

## Output expectation

- ICP and buyer map
- sales motion recommendation
- value and ROI framing
- likely objections
- required sales assets
- next experiments or pipeline moves
