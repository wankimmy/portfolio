# Operations Playbook

Use this for SOPs, vendor management, change management, capacity planning, handoffs, RACI, and recurring operating cadence.

## Workflow

1. Name the operating outcome and trigger.
2. Identify stakeholders: owner, operator, approver, affected teams, and escalation path.
3. Map the current flow from trigger to completion.
4. Mark invisible work, handoffs, waiting states, approvals, exception paths, and rework loops.
5. Capture constraints: budget, compliance, vendor terms, staffing, tooling, customer commitments, and data risk.
6. Design the smallest target process with inputs, outputs, evidence, cadence or SLA, and drift review.
7. Turn the result into the right artifact: SOP, RACI, runbook, vendor register, decision log, or project board.

## Output expectation

- current-state map
- target process
- owners and escalation
- risks and controls
- smallest next step
