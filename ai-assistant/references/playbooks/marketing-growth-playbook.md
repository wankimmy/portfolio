# Marketing and Growth Playbook

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

Use this when shaping demand generation, messaging, channels, or growth strategy.

## Workflow

1. Define the audience and buying trigger.
2. Clarify the positioning and message.
3. Select realistic channels for the stage, budget, and product type.
4. Map the funnel from discovery to activation.
5. Identify loops, referrals, content, partnerships, or outbound motions that could compound.

## Output expectation

- target audience
- positioning and message
- chosen channels
- funnel view
- growth experiments
- measurement suggestions
