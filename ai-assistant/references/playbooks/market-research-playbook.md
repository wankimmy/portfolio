# Market Research Playbook

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

Use this when evaluating a market, competitor set, or positioning question.

## Workflow

1. Define the exact market and buyer.
2. Identify direct competitors, substitutes, and alternatives.
3. Check recent shifts that could affect the answer.
4. Compare positioning, pricing, strengths, and weakness patterns.
5. Convert research into implications for product and go-to-market.

## Output expectation

- market scope
- competitor map
- current trend signal
- strategic implications
- confidence level and open gaps
