# Launch Commercialization Playbook

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

Use this when the goal is not just to build the product, but to launch it successfully and turn it into revenue.

## Workflow

1. Assess launch readiness across engineering, UX, trust, and operational basics.
2. Define the ICP, buyer, user, market, and target country or region.
3. Clarify positioning, differentiation, and the purchase-trigger story.
4. Evaluate discoverability through SEO, GEO, content structure, and landing page clarity.
5. Select the monetization model, pricing shape, and sales motion that fit the market and stage.
6. Recommend a realistic marketing plan and a realistic sales plan for the chosen region.
7. Identify what evidence is needed to judge progress toward product-market fit.
8. Produce a phased launch plan with immediate next steps.

## Output expectation

- launch readiness assessment
- ICP and market/country strategy
- positioning and SEO/GEO angle
- marketing plan
- sales strategy
- monetization recommendation
- PMF signals and risks
- phased launch plan
