# Product Spec Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

- Is the user problem explicit and narrow enough to act on?
- Is the target user defined?
- Is the core workflow described step by step?
- Are acceptance criteria concrete and testable?
- Are non-goals stated?
- Are dependencies and assumptions listed?
- Are rollout and adoption risks addressed?
- Are success metrics or leading indicators defined?
