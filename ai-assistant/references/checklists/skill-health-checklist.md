# Skill Health Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

- Does the skill have a clear trigger instead of a vague “use this for anything” description?
- Does it add unique value that is not already covered by `AGENTS.md`, memory, a playbook, or another skill?
- Is the workflow actionable enough to change how the assistant works, not just describe a topic?
- Is the scope tight enough to avoid overlap with neighboring skills?
- Are the references still the right ones, and do they match files that actually exist in the repo?
- Would merging, trimming, or promoting part of the content reduce maintenance cost without losing value?
- If the skill is weak or stale, is there a specific better artifact it should merge into or be replaced by?
- Did you avoid deleting or retiring anything without an explicit reason and safer replacement path?
