# Lead Intelligence Checklist

## Pre-work
- [ ] Lead type defined: customer / investor / partner / hire
- [ ] ICP criteria listed: industry, company size, title, geography, tech stack, stage
- [ ] Outreach goal defined: meeting / intro / demo / partnership discussion
- [ ] Channel selected: email / LinkedIn / warm intro / event

## Discovery
- [ ] Search queries designed for Exa (semantic, not just keyword)
- [ ] LinkedIn signals checked: recent posts, job changes, funding announcements
- [ ] Intent signals identified: content engagement, job postings, product reviews
- [ ] Warm path checked: mutual connections, shared communities, alumni networks

## Scoring
- [ ] Fit score assigned (ICP match: 1–5)
- [ ] Timing score assigned (intent signals: 1–5)
- [ ] Warm path score assigned (connection quality: 1–5)
- [ ] Prioritized by total score ≥ 10

## Outreach
- [ ] Personalization hook identified (specific to this lead)
- [ ] Message < 100 words (first touch)
- [ ] Clear single CTA (not multiple asks)
- [ ] Follow-up sequence planned (max 3 touches)

## Quality gate
- [ ] No spray-and-pray: every message personalized
- [ ] Unsubscribe / opt-out respected
- [ ] CRM or tracking sheet updated after each touch
