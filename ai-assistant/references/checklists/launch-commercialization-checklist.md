# Launch Commercialization Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

- Is the product actually ready enough to sell, or are there engineering, UX, trust, or reliability gaps that would hurt conversion?
- What is the target country or region, and how does that change language, pricing, payment methods, compliance, buyer behavior, and channels?
- Who is the ICP, buyer, champion, user, and purchase trigger?
- What positioning, SEO angle, GEO answerability, and proof points make the offer attractive enough to read and buy?
- What is the monetization model: pricing, packaging, free tier, trial, usage-based, services-assisted, or enterprise?
- What does product-market-fit evidence currently look like, and what evidence is still missing?
- What launch channels, content, campaigns, outreach, partnerships, or sales motions are realistic for the current stage?
- What assets are required before launch: landing page, demo, onboarding, pricing page, FAQ, case studies, comparison pages, outbound messaging?
- What metrics matter across acquisition, activation, conversion, revenue, retention, and qualitative feedback?
- What is the smallest country-specific launch plan that can validate demand before expanding?
