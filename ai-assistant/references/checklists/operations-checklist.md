# Operations Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files, ask clarifying yes/no questions before acting.

- Is the operating area clear: vendor, SOP, change management, capacity, cadence, incident follow-up, or handoff?
- Is the trigger, output artifact, owner, operator, approver, and escalation path named?
- Are handoffs, waiting states, rework loops, and exception paths visible?
- Are cost, SLA, compliance, security, staffing, and vendor-exit risks considered?
- Is the process lightweight enough for the actual recurrence and risk?
- Are adoption signals, review cadence, and drift checks defined?
- Is the smallest next operational step clear?
