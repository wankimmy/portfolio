# Marketing and Growth Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

- Who is the buyer and what pain are they already trying to solve?
- What message is clear enough to repeat consistently?
- Which channels are realistic for this stage and budget?
- What is the wedge or hook that gets initial attention?
- What conversion path exists from discovery to activation?
- What retention or loop could compound growth?
- Which assumptions about demand, channel fit, or willingness to pay still need evidence?
