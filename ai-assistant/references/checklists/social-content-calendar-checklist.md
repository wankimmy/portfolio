# Social Content Calendar Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

- Which platforms matter most for this audience right now: LinkedIn, X, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, Reddit, email, or community channels?
- What content pillars should repeat across the month: education, proof, founder story, product demo, objection handling, case study, launch updates?
- What timezone, country, and working-week assumptions should drive the posting schedule?
- What posting cadence is realistic to sustain without creating low-quality filler?
- For each platform, what format fits best: short text, carousel, image, reel, short video, thread, long-form post, live session?
- What hook makes each post worth stopping to read?
- What CTA should each post drive: comment, DM, signup, demo request, waitlist, trial, or purchase?
- What proof points or trust signals should be included regularly?
- Which dates, launches, events, or seasonal angles should the calendar align with?
- What metrics matter by platform: impressions, saves, clicks, replies, DMs, signups, demos, purchases?
