# Market notes

Use this file for **durable market-level learnings** that should inform positioning, pricing, packaging, or channel choices across sessions — not day-to-day news.

## When to add an entry

Add when the insight is likely to stay valid for **6+ months** and you would reuse it on a **future** strategy or launch task:

- Competitor **positioning or packaging** patterns (not a single pricing screenshot).
- **Recurring buyer objections** or proof points that closed deals.
- **Stable channel economics** you have measured (blended CAC bands, conversion benchmarks) with date and segment noted.

Do **not** store: hype cycles without enduring implication, unverified rumors, or customer-specific confidential terms.

## When to promote out

- **Repeating GTM lesson** → **marketing-growth** / **sales-strategy** skill or playbook note, or **launch-commercialization** checklist.
- **Entire category of competitor behavior** → **market-analysis** workflow or `learning-log.md` with a pointer here.

## Suggested format (append new blocks)

```markdown
### [Theme] — YYYY-MM

- **Context:** segment / geography
- **Observation:** what is true in the market
- **Implication:** what we should do differently
- **Confidence:** high / medium / low — **verify by:** [source or next research step]
```
