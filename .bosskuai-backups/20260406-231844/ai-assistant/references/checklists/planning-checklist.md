# Planning Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

- Is the goal specific enough to sequence?
- What is the smallest meaningful delivery slice?
- What must happen first, and what can wait?
- What dependencies or blockers exist?
- What is risky, irreversible, or hard to unwind?
- What needs validation before more build effort is spent?
- What does a realistic next 30, 60, or 90 days look like?
- What should be explicitly deferred?
