# Paid Acquisition and Monetization Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

- What is the offer, who is it for, and what action should paid traffic take?
- Which country or region is in scope, and how does that affect CPC, channel fit, language, compliance, and payment behavior?
- What is the monetization model: subscription, usage-based, one-time purchase, services-assisted, freemium, hybrid?
- What is the acceptable CAC relative to LTV, payback period, or margin?
- Which paid channels are realistic: Google Search, Google Display, YouTube, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, Reddit, influencer sponsorships?
- What keyword intent, audience segment, or funnel stage should each campaign target?
- What landing pages, offers, and trust signals are needed before spending?
- What conversion events should be tracked: lead, signup, booked demo, trial start, purchase, retained customer?
- What budget, test horizon, and stop-loss rules should be used?
- What pricing or packaging experiments should run in parallel with acquisition?
