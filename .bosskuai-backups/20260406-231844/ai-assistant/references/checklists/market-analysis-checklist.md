# Market Analysis Checklist

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

- What exact market or segment is being discussed?
- Which competitors are direct, adjacent, or substitute products?
- What is changing recently that could affect the answer?
- What evidence supports demand, pain, urgency, and willingness to pay?
- What does this imply for positioning, pricing, or wedge strategy?
- Which conclusions are confirmed versus inferred?
