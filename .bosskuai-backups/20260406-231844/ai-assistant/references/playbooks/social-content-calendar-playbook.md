# Social Content Calendar Playbook

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

Use this when the goal is to produce a platform-specific content plan that a founder or team can copy and follow directly.

## Workflow

1. Identify the audience, country, timezone, and the platforms that actually matter.
2. Define a small number of content pillars tied to business goals.
3. Choose a realistic posting cadence by platform.
4. Build a dated posting schedule with suggested day, local time, format, hook, topic, and CTA.
5. Adapt the message for each platform instead of duplicating the exact same post everywhere.
6. Include measurement guidance and the next iteration loop.

## Output expectation

- platform priorities
- content pillars
- weekly or monthly posting calendar
- date and local posting time
- post format and CTA
- measurement plan
