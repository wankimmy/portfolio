# Legal / Compliance Playbook

> If the request is general, ambiguous, or touches many files — ask clarifying yes/no questions **before acting**. Use numbered bullets with explicit answer format: e.g. `1-yes/no  2-A/B`.

Use this when reviewing privacy posture, consent, retention, vendor obligations, or product-policy alignment.

## Workflow

1. Read the product flow, data handling behavior, and any relevant policy or vendor docs.
2. Identify likely obligation areas: privacy, consent, retention, transfer, contract, or market-specific rules.
3. Compare real product behavior against stated policy and expected obligations.
4. Identify what needs qualified legal/compliance escalation before shipping.
5. Recommend the smallest immediate hardening or review step.

## Output expectation

- compliance surface summary
- obligation areas in scope
- policy/product gaps
- escalation triggers
- next compliance-hardening step
